About Me

My Story


I grew up in Weston Connecticut and attended Syracuse University for industrial and interactive design.


I have prior experience both as a designer and working in health care. I have worked for a design studio creating packaging and bottle designs for beauty products. There, I learned how to design products that were both beautiful and functional. The marriage of form and function are design principles that I currently use when creating user interfaces. In recent years I have worked at a treatment center to help patients overcome addiction and mental health issues. My prior design experience has helped grow my artistic and creative skills while my health care experience has shown me how to work with people in a compassionate manner.


Outside of work I enjoy staying active, exercising, skiing, and hiking. I also have a strong passion for the fine arts. I love to draw, paint, and sculpt.
